About

With locations sprinkled throughout San Diego, we at Fernani are committed to serving high-quality, locally roasted coffee and baked goods. We believe that your experience in Fernani is just as important your coffee, and hope you can stay and enjoy our friendly space. Roasting our coffee in San Diego and sourcing all other items for Fernani locally, we are committed to growing the community that has lovingly embraced us.
